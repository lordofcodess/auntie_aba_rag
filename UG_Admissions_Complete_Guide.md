# University of Ghana
Complete Undergraduate Admissions Guide — 2025/2026
Source: admissions.ug.edu.gh | Verified March 2026

## 1. General Entry Requirements (WASSCE/SSSCE)

Every applicant must have **credit passes (A1-C6 WASSCE or A-D SSSCE)** in six subjects: 4 core + 3 electives relevant to the programme.

**Core Subjects (all 4 required):** English Language | Core Mathematics | Integrated Science | Social Studies

# 5. How to Apply — Step by Step

## Buy Your E-Voucher
Cost: GHS 220.00 for Ghanaian applicants | US$110 for International applicants.
USSD: Dial *889*9# on any network (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo).
1. Or visit any of these banks: Zenith Bank, Universal Merchant Bank, Access Bank, Ecobank, Republic Bank, Agricultural Development Bank (ADB), Consolidated Bank Ghana, GCB Bank, First Atlantic Bank, Fidelity Bank. Provide your full name, phone number and email. Your Serial Number and PIN will be sent by SMS. KEEP YOUR VOUCHER SAFE — you need it to check your admission status later.

## Log Into the Portal
2. Go to https://admissions.ug.edu.gh and log in with the Serial Number and PIN from your e-voucher. International applicants: https://apply.ug.edu.gh/admissions/igraduate/login

## Complete the Application Form

# 7. Special Admission Categories

**Mature Applicants:** Typically 25 years and above. May be required to sit entrance exams or attend interviews. Mature student admission available for BA Distance Education (re-introduced from 2017/2018). Must satisfy UG mature entry conditions — different cut-off rules apply.

**International Applicants:** Accepted qualifications: A-Levels (3 relevant subjects + 5 O-Level passes incl. English & Maths), IB Diploma (Grade 4+ in 3 HL subjects), or equivalent. Application fee: US$110 payable via Ecobank wire transfer or Visa/MasterCard at https://sts.ug.edu.gh/ugpay. Apply at: https://apply.ug.edu.gh/admissions/igraduate/login

**Diploma / HND to Degree:** Holders of a recognized diploma or HND may enter at Level 200 or Level 300. Minimum CGPA requirements apply. Must purchase standard e-voucher (GHS 220) and apply via portal.

**SSSCE Applicants:** Grade scale: A-D (A=1, B=2, C=3, D=4). Aggregate calculated the same way as WASSCE. Note: UG stopped admitting O-Level and A-Level certificate holders from 2007/2008.

**Previous UG Students:** If you previously attended or graduated from UG from 1996 to date, include your Student Identity Number on the application form. The same number will be used if admitted.
